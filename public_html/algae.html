Introduction To Algae

Algae are a type of eukaryote in the protist world. They are mainly aquatic and have fixed cell walls, and because they have chlorophyll, they are also considered plant-like organisms.

In the food chain

They are the first-level producers. Without them, we will face severe food shortages.
They are also responsible for the fishy smell or eutrophication
Some algae like dinoflagellate are highly toxic and have serious consequences for the body.
